About me

My name is Lou Ann Coulon and I’m 21 years old. After a scientific high school diploma, I chose to study graphic design, a field that had been interesting me for years.

I am currently a fourth year student at ECV - École de Communication Visuelle in Bordeaux, France and I am studying to obtain a Corporate Design Master's degree. I also work as a freelance graphic designer for the french brand Monsieur TSHIRT.

I’m passionate about illustration and typography, I like to combine those two skills in my creations to apply them to graphic design.
